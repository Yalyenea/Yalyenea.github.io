About Me

Hi, I’m yfff, a master’s student.

I’m interested in AI/ML/DL/LLM, optimization, and signal processing. More broadly, I care about how intelligent systems learn from data, how optimization shapes that learning process, and how signal processing helps reveal structure in complex observations.

I enjoy understanding ideas from first principles, especially when they connect elegant theory with practical systems. This blog is a place for my notes, thoughts, and experiments from that ongoing exploration.
