During the May Day holiday I had headaches for five or six straight days. Looking at the left side of the screen made my eye uncomfortable, with a pulsing pain behind the eye and a pulling sensation. I suspect muscle fatigue from too much screen time and staying up late. I spent almost the whole holiday lying in bed sleeping and did almost nothing. I really need to pay attention to health, eye care, and rest. It is awful when everything feels bad and sleep is the only option; soon after waking up each day, the headache would start again. From now on I want to gradually build better routines: sleep and wake on time, use my eyes scientifically, reduce screen time, go outdoors more, and eat healthier.
