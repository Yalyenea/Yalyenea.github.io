The fgruler package can draw rulers in the page foreground. The example uses:

\usepackage[type={alledges,alledgesT}, color=blue]{fgruler}

Here alledges means drawing rulers on all four page edges, alledgesT means the four edges use the “T” style, and color=blue sets the ruler color to blue.
